# Columbus Dog Treat Trail — Submission Form Copy

## Intro blurb (top of form)

Found a dog treat stash, stick library, water bowl, or toy box in Columbus?
Add it to the map! Quick note before you submit — a few things help us keep
the map useful and up to date:

- **It should be easy to find.** If there's no sign or obvious structure,
  include a landmark in your notes (e.g. "blue mailbox next to the porch").
- **It should be usable most of the year**, not just during summer or the
  holidays. Seasonal stops are welcome too — just flag them as seasonal below.
- **If it's at a business**, it needs to be free, out any time they're open,
  and an actual designated stop (not just a jar someone might move).
- **Keep it family- and dog-friendly.**

Stops that don't quite fit these might still be great — just let us know in
the notes and we'll take a look.

## Confirmation message (after submit)

Thanks! Your stop is in the review queue. Once it's geocoded and checked
against the map's criteria, it'll show up on the Dog Treat Trail as
**unverified** until someone confirms it in person. Walked past it yourself?
Use the "Mark verified" button on its map pin next time you're there.
