Both are roadmap ideas from the maintainer — not live features yet.
Member management
Admin ability to add, edit, remove, or ban signed-in members (join.gs' Members sheet) — currently there's no way to revoke, correct, or block an account once created via Google sign-in.
Business support
A support channel for sponsoring businesses (promo setup help, billing questions, pin changes) — right now the only path is the maintainer's personal email, with no ticket history.
Community chat
A space for walkers to help one another — flag a stop that's gone stale, swap route tips, coordinate a group walk — instead of everything routing through the maintainer.
Promo redemption metrics
A way for sponsoring businesses to see whether their offer is actually being redeemed at checkout — right now a sponsored pin has no feedback loop back to the business at all.
